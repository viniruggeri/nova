Deciphering Complexity

Core Philosophy

Intelligence is not a static property but an emergent phenomenon. We believe that intelligence emerges and organizes in unstable, incomplete, and noisy environments.

Our research program focuses on the intersection of dynamic systems, probabilistic inference, and adaptive reasoning. We reject the notion of intelligence as simple pattern matching, instead modeling it as a continuous process of dynamic inference within uncertain worlds.
